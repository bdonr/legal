## 1. Collection of Information

### 1.1 Information You Provide to Us
We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, participate in features, request support, or otherwise communicate with us.

Examples include:
- Name, email address, and profile information  
- Photos, posts, or other content you choose to share  
- Contact details you submit voluntarily  

### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically
When you access or use our services, we may automatically collect certain information including:

**Log Information**
- Usage data such as access times, pages viewed, IP address, and referring page.

**Device Information**
- Information about your device, operating system, and app version.

### 1.3 Location Information
CTWOME uses location data to enable features that help users discover people and activity in their surrounding area.

When a user activates the **Live Radar** feature, the app may access the device’s location to determine nearby users and update the user’s approximate presence within the network.

Location data may be collected **while the app is running in the background or when the device screen is locked**, but only while the user has enabled the Live Radar feature.

This supports features such as:
- **Live Radar ("Who is around")** – showing nearby users who have chosen to participate  
- **Networking in daily life** – enabling users to connect with people they encounter in real-world environments  
- **Heatmap** – showing areas with higher activity levels  
- **Treasure Hunt & Rewards** – allowing users to collect coins or gifts at certain locations  
- **Notifications** – informing users when friends or favorite creators are nearby  

To reduce battery usage and protect privacy:
- Location sharing is **optional**
- Users must **explicitly enable Live Radar**
- The app **does not display precise user locations** to others
- Location updates occur **only when significant movement is detected**
- Users can **disable the feature at any time**

If location permissions are denied or revoked, certain nearby discovery features may not function.

### 1.4 Cookies and Tracking
We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve functionality, analyze usage, and enhance the user experience.

### 1.5 Information From Other Sources
We may combine information from external services (such as authentication providers) with information collected through CTWOME.

## 6. Your Information Choices

### 6.1 Location Information
When you first use location-based features, you will be asked to grant location permissions.

If you allow **background location access**, CTWOME may continue receiving location updates while the app is running in the background in order to support features such as Live Radar.

You may stop location collection at any time by:
- Pressing the **Stop** button inside the app, or
- Changing location permissions in your device settings.

### 6.2 Additional User Controls
You remain in control of your privacy:
- **Stop button:** disables Live Radar and related location-based features
- **Block / Ignore users:** you can block users you do not want to interact with
- **Ghost Mode / Hide from Heatmap:** you can choose not to appear on the radar or heatmap while still using other features

### 6.3 Cookies
You may disable cookies in your browser settings. However, this may affect certain features of our services.

### 6.4 Promotional Communications
You may opt out of promotional emails at any time. However, we may still send service-related communications necessary for operating the app.
